Delete your account & data

How to permanently remove your PillPal account and all of its data.

This page applies to the PillPal app (com.pillpal.pillpal_mobile). You can delete your account and all associated data in either of the following ways.

Option 1 — delete from inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open PillPal and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Delete account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and all data are deleted immediately and cannot be undone.

Option 2 — request deletion by email (if you’ve uninstalled the app)

Email pillpal54@gmail.com from the address you signed up with, with the subject “Delete my account”. We verify ownership of the email and complete the deletion within 30 days (typically within a few days).

What gets deleted

  • Your account (email / Google identity) and profile.
  • All medicines, schedules, reminder times, and dose-history logs.

Deletion is permanent. We do not keep a backup of your health data after deletion. Limited records required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance may be retained as described in our privacy policy.
